Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 9, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what information Vynix ("we", "us") collects when you use the Vynix Messenger app (the "App"), and how we use, store, and protect it.

1. Information We Collect

CategoryExamples
Account infoName, username, email address, phone number (if linked), profile photo, "about" text
Content you createMessages, media, voice notes, and documents you send through the App
ContactsDevice contacts you choose to sync, to help you find friends already using Vynix
Usage dataOnline status, typing indicators, and read receipts
Payment dataSubscription status and entitlements (handled by our payment provider — we do not see or store your card details)

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide core messaging functionality (delivering messages, showing online status, etc.)
  • To let you find and connect with contacts already on Vynix
  • To process and manage your Verified Badge subscription
  • To maintain the security and integrity of the App
  • To communicate with you about your account or updates to the App

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third parties to operate the App:

  • Google Firebase — authentication, database, file storage, and push notifications.
  • Paddle.com Market Ltd — payment processing and billing for the Verified Badge subscription, acting as merchant of record. Paddle collects and processes your payment details directly; see Paddle's Privacy Policy.
  • RevenueCat — subscription and entitlement management, to determine which features you have access to.

4. Data Retention

We retain your account and message data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we delete or anonymize your personal data within a reasonable period, except where retention is required for legal or billing purposes.

5. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information, or to object to certain processing. To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@vynix.co.za.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13 (or the applicable minimum age in your country), and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under that age.

7. Security

We use industry-standard measures to protect your data, but no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. We encourage you to use a strong, unique password for your account.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We'll notify you of material changes by updating the "Last updated" date above.

9. Contact

Questions about this Privacy Policy? Contact us at privacy@vynix.co.za.
