Pricing FAQ

How do I subscribe?

Open the Vynix app, go to Settings → Premium, and tap "Get Verified" to complete your purchase securely in-app.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Cancelling stops future billing — you'll keep your badge until the end of the period you already paid for.

Who processes my payment?

Payments are securely handled by Paddle.com Market Ltd, our payment provider and merchant of record. Vynix never sees or stores your card details.

Do you offer refunds?

Subscription payments are non-refundable. See our Refund Policy for full details.
