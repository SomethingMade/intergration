Refund Policy

Last updated: July 9, 2026

This Refund Policy applies to the Verified Badge subscription purchased within the Vynix Messenger app (the "Subscription").

1. No Refunds

Payments for the Subscription are non-refundable. Because the Verified Badge grants immediate access to digital features upon payment, we do not offer refunds or credits for any completed billing period, including partial months, unused time, or if you forget to cancel before renewal.

2. Cancelling Your Subscription

You can cancel your Subscription at any time. Cancelling stops future renewals — you'll keep access to Verified Badge features until the end of the billing period you already paid for, after which the Subscription will not renew and no further charges will occur.

3. Exceptions

We may issue a refund at our discretion in cases such as a duplicate charge, a proven billing error, or where required by applicable law in your jurisdiction. To request this, contact us at support@vynix.co.za with your account details and the reason for your request.

4. Payment Processor

Subscriptions are billed by Paddle.com Market Ltd, our payment provider and merchant of record. Any refund approved under this policy is processed by Paddle back to your original payment method.

5. Your Statutory Rights

Nothing in this policy limits any refund or cancellation rights you're entitled to under applicable consumer protection law in your country of residence.

6. Contact

Questions about billing or this policy? Contact us at support@vynix.co.za.
