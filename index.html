Terms & Policies

1. Introduction

Welcome to Pace Parcels. By accessing or using our application, you agree to be bound by these Terms and Conditions. Please read them carefully.

2. Service Description

Pace Parcels provides a platform connecting users with independent drivers for local parcel delivery. We act as a technology intermediary, not a direct logistics provider.

3. User Responsibilities

You must provide accurate pickup and delivery details. You agree not to send illegal, hazardous, or heavily restricted items through our service. You must ensure you are reachable on your provided mobile number during a delivery.

4. Payments and Refunds

Fares are estimated upfront based on distance and package size. Actual charges may vary slightly in the event of major route diversions. Refund requests are evaluated on a case-by-case basis by our support team.

5. Liability and Insurance

While we strive for completely safe and secure deliveries, Pace Parcels' liability is limited. High-value items must be properly packaged, declared, and independently insured where applicable.

6. Privacy Policy & POPIA

We process your personal information strictly in accordance with the Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA), Act 4 of 2013. Your data (name, location, phone number) is used only to facilitate deliveries, verify driver identity, and improve your app experience.
