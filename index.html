LATCH · IDENTITY VERIFICATION

Know who you're hiring, before day one

Latch verifies the identity of every new hire during onboarding — document checks, biometric matching, and a compliance-ready record, all through a single API call.

Get Latch for Your Team

Document Verification

New hires scan a passport, driver's license, or national ID. We extract and validate the data and check the document for authenticity across a broad range of countries and formats.

Liveness & Face Match

A quick selfie confirms the person completing onboarding is the same person on the document — not a stolen identity, a photo, or a deepfake.

Proof of Address

For remote and distributed hires, confirm where someone actually lives — useful for payroll, tax, and jurisdiction-specific compliance requirements.

Compliance Dashboard

Every check produces a timestamped, storable record. HR and compliance teams get a single dashboard to track verification status across every hire.
